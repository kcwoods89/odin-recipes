Lasagna

Description

Here is a simple recipe for lasagna that will make the whole family happy! Just follow the steps below for dinner tonight!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Your pasta sauce of choice
  • Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Bring large pot of water to boil.
  2. Preheat oven to 375F.
  3. Once water is boiling add noodles and cook until tender.
  4. Add sauce to bottom of large baking pan.
  5. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese in pan.
  6. Bake for 10 - 15 minutes until top is brown.
  7. Allow to cool for 10 minutes.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
